INCIDENT SUMMARY:

On July 28, 2002, at 3:32 AM, the Emett County Police Department responded to a call made about a body found in a wooded area behind a Magnolia Blvd. Apartment complex. The corpse was described as severely decomposed and tampered, immediately suggesting foul play.

Emmet County Police and Emett County Fire Deparment identified the body as previously missing woman Cathy Hem Mitchell, and was pronounced dead at 3:45 AM. Preliminary examination revealed blunt force trauma to the head and upper torso. Cathy's cellphone was recovered nearby in the dirt.

No suspects were located on site. There were no witnesses present.
